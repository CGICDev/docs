<Info>
  **Prerequisites**

  Before calculating:

  1. **Active BOMs**: For all products (e.g., _Onina Tissue BOM_ with pulp, adhesive, packaging).
  2. **Accurate Stock**: Update raw material quantities in `Stock > Stock Balance`.
  3. **Substitutes**: Define alternates (e.g., _Yeast-Brand-B_ for Valdana Bread).
</Info>

### **1.0 Overview**

**Material Requirements Planning (MRP)** calculates total raw materials needed based on:

- **Production Quantity** (e.g., _5,000 Valdana Bread loaves_)
- **Bill of Materials (BOM)**
- **Current Stock Levels**
- **Planned Substitutions**

### **2. 0 Step-by-Step Calculation**

**2.1 Generate Production Plan**

1. Go to: `Manufacturing > Production Plan > New`
2. Fill in:
   - **Item**: Finished product (e.g., _Luca Engine Oil 5W-30_)
   - **Quantity**: Production target (e.g., _1,000 liters_)
   - **Use Multi-Level BOM**: ✓ Check if using sub-assemblies
3. **Get Raw Materials**:
   - System auto-calculates requirements based on BOM.

#### **2.2 Review Material Requests**

1. **Raw Materials Table** shows:
   - **Required Qty**: Total materials needed (e.g., _950L base oil for 1,000L Luca Oil_)
   - **Available Qty**: Current stock
   - **Additional Qty**: Shortfall to procure
2. **Consider Substitutes**:
   - If _Additive-X_ is low, system suggests _Additive-Y_ (if defined as alternate).

#### **2.3 Adjust for Waste/Scrap**

In BOM settings:

1. Set **Scrap %** for loss-prone materials:
   - _Example_: 5% scrap rate for Ovita Water caps → system adds extra 5% to requirement.

### **3.0 CGIC Product Examples**

#### **Valdana Bread (1,000 loaves)**

| **Material** | **BOM Qty/Loaf** | **Total Required** | **Available** | **To Order**               |
| :----------- | :--------------- | :----------------- | :------------ | :------------------------- |
| Flour        | 0.5 kg           | 500 kg             | 300 kg        | 200 kg                     |
| Yeast        | 0.02 kg          | 20 kg              | 5 kg          | 15 kg (_or Yeast-Brand-B_) |
| Sugar        | 0.1 kg           | 100 kg             | 120 kg        | 0 kg                       |

#### **Ovita Waters (5,000 bottles)**

| **Material**   | **BOM Qty/Bottle** | **Total Required**         |
| :------------- | :----------------- | :------------------------- |
| Bottles        | 1                  | 5,000                      |
| Caps           | 1                  | 5,000 (\+5% scrap = 5,250) |
| Purified Water | 0.5L               | 2,500L                     |
