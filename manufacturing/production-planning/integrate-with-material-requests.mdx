## **Steps to Integrate Production with Material Requests**

### **Step 1: Auto-Generate MRs from Production Plan**

1. Open **Production Plan** (e.g., _"Valdana Bread Batch #24"_)
2. Click **Get Raw Materials** â†’ System calculates shortages:

   | **Material** | **Required** | **Available** | **Shortage** |
   | :----------- | :----------- | :------------ | :----------- |
   | Flour        | 500 kg       | 300 kg        | 200 kg       |
   | Yeast        | 20 kg        | 5 kg          | 15 kg        |
3. Click **Create Material Request**:
   - **MR Type**: "Purchase"
   - **Set Priority**:
     - _High_ (for critical items like yeast)
     - _Medium/Low_ (for non-urgent items)
4. **Submit** â†’ MRs auto-generated with:
   - **Production Plan link**
   - **Required By Date** (aligned with production schedule)

### **Step 2: Track MR Status in Production Plan**

1. Reopen **Production Plan** â†’ **Raw Materials Table**:
   - New column: **Material Request Status**

     | Material | Shortage | MR Status     |
     | -------- | -------- | ------------- |
     | Flour    | 200 kg   | âœ… PO Created  |

2. Click MR links to:
   - View procurement progress
   - Resubmit if rejected

### **Step 3: Receive Materials Against MR**

1. When goods arrive:
   - Create **Purchase Receipt** (`Stock > Purchase Receipt > New`)
2. **Link to MR**:
   - Select MR (e.g., _"MR-YEAST-024"_)
   - System auto-populates items/quantities
3. **Receive Stock**:
   - Warehouse: _Raw Material Warehouse_
   - Batch/Expiry: Record for perishables (e.g., yeast)
4. **Submit** â†’ Production Plan updates:
   - **Available Qty** increases
