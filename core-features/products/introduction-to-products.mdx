---
title: "Introduction to Products"
description: "The Products module in the CGIC App provides end‐to‐end management of your inventory items—defining, organizing, tracking stock, pricing, vendor relationships, and movement—ensuring accurate data for operations and finance."
---

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Add Product" icon="circle-plus" iconType="duotone" href="https://cgic.mintlify.app/core-features/products/add-product">
    - Create new product records with SKU, name, description, unit of measure, default warehouse, and tax classification.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Edit Product" icon="heart-circle-minus" iconType="duotone" href="https://cgic.mintlify.app/core-features/products/edit-product">
    - Update product attributes—specifications, units, reorder levels, barcode—and adjust settings as your catalog evolves.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Remove Product" icon="delete-left" iconType="duotone" href="https://cgic.mintlify.app/core-features/products/delete-product">
    - Delete obsolete or duplicate products safely, ensuring no active transactions reference them.
  </Card>
  <Card title="View Product" icon="eye" iconType="duotone" href="https://cgic.mintlify.app/core-features/products/view-product">
    - Open a product’s profile to see details: stock summary, pricing tiers, vendor links, and recent movements.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Search Products" icon="magnifying-glass-location" iconType="duotone" href="https://cgic.mintlify.app/core-features/products/search-products">
    - Quickly locate items by SKU, name, category, or custom fields using global search and advanced filters.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Manage Categories" icon="clock-eight" iconType="duotone" href="https://cgic.mintlify.app/core-features/products/manage-categories">
    - Organize products into hierarchical categories for easier reporting, navigation, and bulk operations.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Track Stock Levels" icon="train-track" iconType="duotone" href="https://cgic.mintlify.app/core-features/products/track-stock-levels">
    - Monitor real‐time on‐hand, reserved, and available quantities across all warehouses and stores.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Sync Orders Inventory" icon="object-group" iconType="duotone" href="https://cgic.mintlify.app/core-features/products/sync-orders-inventory">
    - Automatically adjust stock when sales or purchase orders are submitted, keeping inventory counts accurate.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Update Pricing" icon="arrow-up-from-square" iconType="duotone" href="https://cgic.mintlify.app/core-features/products/update-pricing">
    - Maintain base prices, cost prices, and markup rules; apply bulk price updates or import via CSV.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Manage Vendors" icon="people-dress-simple" iconType="duotone" href="https://cgic.mintlify.app/core-features/products/manage-vendors">
    - Link products to supplier records, define preferred vendor rates, lead times, and minimum order quantities.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Financial Product Data" icon="database" iconType="duotone" href="https://cgic.mintlify.app/core-features/products/financial-product-data">
    - Provide stock valuation, cost of goods sold, and inventory aging details for accounting and financial reporting.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Transfer Inventory" icon="file-invoice" iconType="duotone" href="https://cgic.mintlify.app/core-features/products/transfer-inventory">
    - Create and track stock transfers between stores and warehouses, with status updates and reconciliation.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Track Inbound Products" icon="chart-mixed" iconType="duotone" href="https://cgic.mintlify.app/core-features/products/track-inbound-products">
    - Log goods received from manufacturing or returns, updating stock and linking to production orders or RMA.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Track Outbound Products" icon="aperture" iconType="duotone" href="https://cgic.mintlify.app/core-features/products/track-outbound-products">
    - Record items shipped—to distributors, end‐customers, or disposed due to damage/expiry—updating stock and order status.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Product Movement Log" icon="chart-radar" iconType="duotone" href="https://cgic.mintlify.app/core-features/products/product-movement-log">
    - Maintain an audit trail of every stock transaction per product, showing date, type, quantity, and user.
  </Card>
