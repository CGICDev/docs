<Info>
  Prerequisites\
  \
  Your raw material exists as an **Item** under **Stock ▶️ Item ▶️ Item List**
</Info>

### 1.0 Steps to Update a Raw Material

1. **Open the Item List**
   - From the home page, go to **Stock ▶️ Item ▶️ Item List**.
   - Locate your raw material (e.g. “Premium Wheat Flour”) using the search bar or filters.
2. **Enter Edit Mode**
   - Click the item’s name to open its profile.
   - Click **Actions ▶️ Edit** (or simply the **Edit** button) in the top-right corner.
3. **Modify Basic Details**
   - On the **Details** tab, update fields such as:
     - **Item Name** or **Item Code**
     - **Item Group** (e.g., switch from “Raw Materials” to a sub-group)
     - **Default UOM** or **Stock UOM**
     - **Description**
4. **Adjust Inventory Settings**
   - Switch to the **Inventory** tab.
   - Change parameters like:
     - **Default Warehouse** (new storage location)
     - **Shelf Life** or **End of Life** dates
     - **Valuation Method** (FIFO ↔ Moving Average)
     - **Allow Negative Stock** toggle
5. **Update Purchasing Info**
   - Click the **Purchasing** tab.
   - In **Supplier Details**, edit or add:
     - **Supplier**, **Lead Time**, **Last Purchase Rate**
     - Mark a different vendor as **Default** if needed
     - Toggle **Allow Purchase**
6. **Revise Accounting Settings**
   - Select the **Accounting** tab.
   - Update:
     - **Expense Account** or **Stock Adjustment Account**
     - **Stock Received But Not Billed** account
     - Any tax or costing variants
7. **Refresh Attachments & Images**
   - If you need a new spec sheet or updated photo, click **Attachments ▶️ Add Attachment** or **Change** on the image placeholder.
8. **Save Your Changes**
   - Click **Save** in the top-right.
