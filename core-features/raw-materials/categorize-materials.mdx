description: "Organize your raw materials into logical groups in the CGIC App to improve searchability, reporting, and procurement planning. "
---

### 1.0 Create or Edit Item Groups

1. **Open Item Group List**
   - Navigate to **Stock▶️ Item Group**.
2. **Add a New Group**
   - Click **New** in the top-right.
   - Enter **Group Name** (e.g., “Grains & Flours”).
   - Select **Parent Group** (leave blank for top-level or choose “Raw Materials”).
   - Check **Is Group** if you plan to add sub-categories.
   - Click **Save**.
3. **Edit an Existing Group**
   - In the Item Group List, click a group name.
   - Update **Group Name**, **Parent Group**, or **Is Group**.
   - Click **Save**.

### 2.0 Assign Raw Materials to Groups

1. **Open the Raw Material**
   - Go to **Stock ▶️ Item List**, filter **Item Group = Raw Materials**, and click the material name.
2. **Change Item Group**
   - On the **Details** tab, set **Item Group** to the desired category (e.g., “Grains & Flours → Wheat Products”).
   - Click **Save**.
3. **Bulk Update via Data Import** (optional)
   - Download the **Item** import template under **Data Import**.
   - Fill in **Item Code** and new **Item Group** columns.
   - Re-import to update multiple raw materials at once.

### 3.0 Verify Your Categorization

- **Item Group List Report**
  - View **Stock ▶️ Reports ▶️ Item Group Summary** to see counts per category.
- **Filtered Item List**
  - In **Item List**, apply the **Item Group** filter to confirm correct assignments.
